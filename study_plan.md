- Backtracking (Problems & Variations) 💡

- DSA Contest 3: Math, Two Pointers, Backtracking, Linked List & Trees 🏆
- Practice in Real Contests 🏅

> **End Goal:** Prepare for interviews, Olympiads, and coding competitions.
